The Religion of Islam

Islam: Islam is a faith based on belief in one God (Allah) and the teachings of Prophet Muhammad.
The Quran is the holy book of Islam. Muslims follow the five pillars of Islam: the declaration of faith,
prayer, fasting during Ramadan, charity, and pilgrimage (Hajj). Islam emphasizes justice, compassion,
and good deeds, and people are judged based on their actions in the afterlife.
